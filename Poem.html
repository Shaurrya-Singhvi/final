Song of the Nature

Verdant trees and bushes; magnificent rivers and seas.

The glorious sunrise, the magical twilight and the cool night breeze.

To the tunes of nature’s melody.
The flowers are dancing,
To the songs of rhapsody.

Dogs are barking, cats are purring,
Lions are roaring, duck are quacking
Animals call nature its abode,
Unfortunately trees getting lesser and buildings getting more,

Nature is showering us with its blessings,
Now it is up to us to protect its wellbeing.
We should pledge to save mother nature,
For health and happiness or our future.
