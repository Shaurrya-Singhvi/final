TRIP TO Bahamas

The Bahamas or The Bahama Islands, is an archipelago consisting of about 2,000 islands if you include the cays, which are small islands that are formed on coral reefs. It has simple people, pristine beaches, robust economy and hair braiding craze. Very popular destination amongst cruise liners and independent tourists as well. Personally I am not a big fan of luxury cruise holidays but Bahamas cruise from Miami on Norwegian sky changed my opinion little bit. Three days filled with best of world food, private islands, unlimited fun and lot to SEE. cruise also help u to see and immerse in lifestyle and culture of people from many many countries.
